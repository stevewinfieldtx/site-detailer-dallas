Ceramic Coating in Dallas

Multi-year nano-ceramic protection that locks in a deep, liquid gloss, repels water and grime, and makes your car radically easier to keep clean — backed by a written warranty.

A ceramic coating is the single best long-term investment for your paint. It's a liquid nano-ceramic that chemically bonds to your factory clear coat and cures into a hard, slick, hydrophobic layer — protecting against Dallas sun, bird droppings, bug acids, water spots, and light chemical staining for years, not weeks.

What a coating actually does for your car

  • Mirror gloss & depth — a wet, candied shine that wax simply can't match or sustain.
  • Hydrophobics — water beads and sheets off, carrying dirt with it. Washing takes half the time.
  • UV & oxidation defense — critical in Texas; keeps reds and blacks from fading and chalking.
  • Chemical resistance — bird droppings and bug guts wipe off before they can etch.

Our ceramic coating process

Coatings are only as good as the prep beneath them — a coating locks in whatever is under it. That's why correction comes first.

1

Decontamination wash

Foam bath, iron/fallout removal, and clay-bar decon to strip the paint of bonded contaminants.

2

Paint correction

Machine polishing to remove swirls and scratches so the coating cures over flawless, glassy paint.

3

Panel wipe & coat

A solvent wipe removes oils, then we hand-apply and level the coating panel by panel.

4

Controlled cure

The car cures in our climate- and dust-controlled bay — the only way to guarantee a flawless finish.

Coating packages & warranty

We offer 2-year, 3–4 year, and 5-year+ packages — more layers and pro-grade product mean a longer warranty. Wheels, glass, trim, and plastics can be coated too. Every coating is registered in your name with a written warranty and a free maintenance guide; we also offer annual inspection top-ups to keep it performing.

Ceramic coating FAQs

How long does it last in Texas heat?

2–5 years depending on the package and care. Our coatings are UV-stable and built for Dallas sun.

Is it worth it over wax?

Wax lasts weeks; a coating lasts years, with stronger gloss, chemical resistance, and hydrophobics that make washing dramatically easier.

Does it stop rock chips?

No — for impact protection, pair ceramic with Paint Protection Film on the front end. Most clients run both.
