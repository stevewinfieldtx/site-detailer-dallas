How much does ceramic coating cost in Dallas?

Short answer: most ceramic coating in Dallas runs $799 to $2,500+. Here's exactly what changes the price — and how to know what your car needs.

Ceramic coating pricing varies more than most services because the prep — not the coating itself — drives most of the cost. Here are the typical 2026 ranges in the Dallas–Fort Worth area:

PackageSedan / coupeSUV / truck
2-year coating (light prep)$799–$1,000$999–$1,300
3–4 year + 1-step correction$1,099–$1,500$1,400–$1,900
5-year+ + multi-stage correction$1,500–$2,200$1,900–$2,500+

What drives the price

  • Paint correction stages. Swirls must be polished out before coating; this is the #1 cost factor.
  • Vehicle size. More panels = more product and labor.
  • Coating tier / warranty length. 5-year products cost more than 2-year.
  • Add-ons. Coating wheels, glass, and trim adds cost but extends protection.

Is it worth it?

For most Dallas drivers, yes — a coating lasts 2–5 years versus weeks for wax, makes washing far easier, and protects resale value against Texas UV and water spotting. The key is honest prep: a cheap "coating" over swirled paint just locks in the defects.
