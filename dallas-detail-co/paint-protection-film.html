Paint Protection Film in Dallas

Self-healing, optically-clear urethane film — "clear bra" — that physically absorbs rock chips and road debris on the most vulnerable parts of your car. Invisible armor for DFW highways.

Get an Instant Quote Call (469) 555-0117

One rock chip on a fresh repaint can cost more than the film. PPF is a thick, self-healing layer applied over your paint that takes the hits so your clear coat doesn't — and on Texas highways full of gravel and construction, it's the smartest insurance you can buy for your paint.

Why PPF beats touch-up paint

  • Self-healing — light swirls and marks in the film vanish with heat from the sun or warm water.
  • Invisible — optically clear; it protects without changing your paint's look.
  • Impact absorption — stops rock chips, road debris, and light scratches cold.
  • 10-year warranty — against yellowing, cracking, and peeling, registered in your name.

Coverage options

Bumper, partial hood, fenders, and mirrors — covers the most common impact zones affordably.

Full front

Full hood, full fenders, bumper, and mirrors — complete protection for the leading edge of the car.

C

Track / full body

High-impact packages or total coverage for exotics, new cars, and anyone who wants zero chips.

PPF + ceramic: the premium combo

PPF and ceramic coating solve different problems — PPF is physical impact protection, ceramic is gloss and easy cleaning. The premium setup runs PPF on the high-impact front end and ceramic over the whole car (it can even go on top of the film for extra slickness).

PPF FAQs

Can you see the film?

No — when installed correctly the edges are wrapped and virtually invisible.

How long does it last?

Quality film lasts 7–10+ years and is backed by a manufacturer warranty.
