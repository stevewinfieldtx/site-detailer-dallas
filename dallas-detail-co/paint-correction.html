Paint Correction in Dallas

Machine polishing that permanently removes swirl marks, scratches, water spots, and oxidation — restoring the true depth and clarity your paint had when it left the factory.

Get an Instant Quote Call (469) 555-0117

If your paint looks hazy, swirled, or dull in the sun, correction is the fix. Those spider-web swirls are thousands of micro-scratches in your clear coat catching light. We level them with machine polishing to bring back a flawless, mirror finish — and it's the essential prep step before any ceramic coating.

What paint correction removes

  • Swirl marks and wash-induced micro-scratching
  • Light to moderate scratches and holograms
  • Water spots and etching from Texas hard water
  • Oxidation, dullness, and faded, chalky paint

1-step vs. multi-stage correction

Correction is paint removal — we level microns of clear coat — so we work conservatively, with paint-depth readings on every panel.

1

1-step enhancement

A single polishing stage that removes roughly 60–70% of defects and dramatically boosts gloss. Ideal for newer or lightly swirled cars.

2

Multi-stage (2–3 step)

Compounding, polishing, and refining for heavily swirled or neglected paint — removes up to ~85–95% of defects.

3

Protect the result

Fresh, corrected paint should be sealed or coated immediately so the clarity lasts. Most clients add a ceramic coating.

What correction can't fix

Correction restores the clear coat, but it can't repair deep scratches that have gone through the clear, stone chips, or dents. We'll tell you honestly what's correctable on a free walkthrough — and never chase a defect at the expense of your paint's health.

Paint correction FAQs

How long does it take?

A 1-step runs a single day; multi-stage correction can take 1–3 days depending on size and condition.

Do I need a coating afterward?

Strongly recommended — corrected paint is bare and unprotected. A ceramic coating locks in the gloss for years.
