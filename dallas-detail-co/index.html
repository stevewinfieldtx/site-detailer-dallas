Good questions

Before you book.

Ceramic coating is a liquid nano-coating that bonds to your paint for years of hydrophobic gloss and easier washing. Paint Protection Film (PPF) is a thick, self-healing film that physically absorbs rock chips and scratches. Many Dallas drivers combine both: PPF on high-impact areas, ceramic over the whole vehicle.

A Signature Detail typically takes 3–5 hours. Paint correction and ceramic coating packages run 1–3 days depending on vehicle condition and the number of correction stages. We'll give you an exact timeline at booking.

Yes — our mobile units serve most of the DFW metro for washes, interiors, and maintenance details. Ceramic coating and PPF are completed in our controlled studio, where dust and temperature control guarantee the best finish.

Our professional coatings last 2–5 years depending on the package and care, and come with a written warranty. You'll get a free maintenance guide, and we offer annual inspection top-ups to keep the warranty active.
